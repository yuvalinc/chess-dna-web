## What changed vs the source

The path geometry (silhouettes, internal detail lines like the rook ring
and king cross) is unchanged from the Cburnett source. Modifications:
- Solid white/black fills replaced with a subtle vertical 2-tone gradient
  for a chess.com Neo-like look
- Outline thinned from `stroke-width: 1.5` to `1.3`
- Soft drop-shadow filter and a ground shadow ellipse added under each piece
- Pieces wrapped in a single `<svg viewBox="0 0 45 45">` per file
